Muscles of hand

Important points

  • All thenar muscles are supplied by median nerve except adductor pollicis and deep head of flexor pollicis brevis
  • All hypothenar muscles are supplied by deep branch of ulnar nerve except palmaris brevis supplied by superficial branch of ulnar nerve
  • All interossei are supplied by deep branch of ulnar nerve
  • The same nerve supplies lumbricals and the tendon from which arises.
  • There are two dorsal interossei for the middle finger
  • The thumb and little finger have their own abductors and hence has no dorsal interossei
  • Palmar interossei adducts (PAD) and dorsal interossei abducts (DAB)
