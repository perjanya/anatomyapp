Coracobrachialis

  • Origin: from tip of coracoid process of scapula
  • Insertion: middle of medial border of humerus
  • Nerve supply: musculocutaneous nerve
  • Action: adduction of arm and weak flexor of arm
  • Functional representation: represents the medial compartment of limb (adductor compartment in lower limb)

Ligament of struthers: the lower extension of coracobrachialis to medial epicondyle or medial supracondylar spur; median nerve and brachial artery passes beneath the arch formed this fibrous band
