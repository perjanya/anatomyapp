Supination and pronation

Definition:

Movement of lower end of radius over ulna in such a way that it crosses it and comes to lie medially is called pronation. With such movement – palm faces downwards with a semiflexed elbow

Movement of lower end of radius over ulna in such a way that it comes to lie parallel to it is called supination. With such movement – palm faces upwards with a semiflexed elbow

Supination and pronation occur at radio-ulnar joints – superior and inferior
