First carpometacarpal joint

= trapeziometacarpal joint

Type

Saddle type of synovial joint

Articulating surfaces

  • Articulating surfaces are reciprocally concavo-convex
  • Imparts a wide range of mobility to the thumb

Ligaments

  • Lateral, anterior and posterior ligaments
  • Palmar and dorsal ligaments are oblique bands

Synovial membrane

Lines the joint capsule and is separate from it

Vascular supply

Radial artery and its first dorsal metacarpal branch

Innervation

Articular twigs from the posterior interosseous nerve

Joint movements

  • Flexion is accompanied by medial rotation
  • Full extension associated with slight lateral rotation
