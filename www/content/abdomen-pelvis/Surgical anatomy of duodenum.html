Surgical anatomy of duodenum

Parts of duodenum

  1. Superior / First part
  2. Descending / Second part
  3. Horizontal / Third part
  4. Ascending / Fourth part

Parts of duodenum

First part of duodenum

= Superior part

2.5 cms

Radiologically - called Duodenal bulb

Attached to hepatoduodenal fold of lesser omentum

At risk relations - posteriorly

First part of duodenum posteriorly is related to

  1. Gastroduodenal artery
  2. Common bile duct
  3. Portal vein

Peptic ulcer: Peptic ulcer eroding the posterior wall of first part of duodenum - Results in significant bleed from gastroduodenal artery
