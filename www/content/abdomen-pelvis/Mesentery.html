Mesentery

Mesentery is a fold of peritoneum suspending part of the gut tube

The mesentery

A fold of peritoneum suspending jejunum and ileum

Free margin

Its free margin suspends jejunum and ileum of nearly 6 meters length

This makes jejunum and ileum to have many frill like folds.

Posterior attachment

It is attached to posterior abdominal wall and is about 6 inches in length.

Crosses

Attachment of the mesentery to posterior abdominal wall –

extends from duodeno-jejunal junction to right sacroiliac joint.

It crosses

  • Third part of duodenum
  • Abdominal aorta and inferior vena cava
  • Right gonadal vessels
  • Right ureter
  • Right psoas muscle

Sigmoid mesocolon

  • Triangular fold of peritoneum
  • Suspends sigmoid colon

Attachment

    • Inverted V shaped
    • Apex at division of common iliac artery
