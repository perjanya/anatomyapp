Anterior Abdominal Wall

Aponeurosis Vs Fascia

Aponeurosis

  • Aponeurosis is a flattened tendon
  • Dense, regular, collagenous connective tissue that joins a muscle to its origin or insertion

Fascia

  • A connective tissue sheath ranges from diffuse to dense
  • Continues over both surfaces of the aponeurosis as the epitendineum
  • Does not have the strength of an aponeurosis
