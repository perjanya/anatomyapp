Female urethra

Female urethra

Length

2 - 4 cm

Location

Directly behind pubic symphysis

Directly over the anterior vaginal wall

Urethra is inseparable from the anterior wall of vagina in distal two third. 

External urethral meatus

Directly above the vaginal opening

External Sphincter Muscle of Female Urethra

Three components

  1. Urethral sphincter - sphincter around the urethra
  2. Compressor urethrae muscle - part of the muscles passing anterior to urethra to ischial rami
  3. Urethrovaginal sphincter - part of the muscle encircling both urethra and vagina
