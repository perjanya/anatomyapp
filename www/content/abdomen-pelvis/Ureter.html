Ureter

Location

Retroperitoneal - placed on posterior abdominal wall

Length

25 cm

Extent

From pelviuretral junction to urinary bladder

Parts

Abdominal part

Up to the pelvic brim

Pelvic part

Beyond pelvic brim

Intravesical or Intramural part

Inside the bladder wall

Constrictions of ureter

5 Constrictions

  • Pelviureteric junction – L2
  • Pelvic brim (sacroiliac joint)
  • Crossing of vas deferens in males, Tunnel in broad ligament in females
  • Intramural part (at ischial spine)
  • Ureteric orifice

Relations – anterior

On right side

  • Second part of duodenum
  • Right colic vessels
  • Ileocolic vessels
  • Right gonadal vessels
  • Root of mesentery

On left side

  • Left colic vessels
  • Sigmoidal vessels
  • Left gonadal vessels
  • Sigmoid mesocolon

Relations – Posterior

Psoas muscle
Genitofemoral nerve
Tips of lumbar transverse processes


At pelvic brim – crosses common iliac vessels

Blood supply

Segmental subadventitial arterial supply from branches of

  1. Renal artery
  2. Inferior suprarenal artery
  3. Gonadal artery
  4. External iliac artery
  5. Internal iliac artery
  6. Inferior vesical artery
  7. Uterine artery

Stricture

Injury to these blood vessels during gynaecological, urological and general surgeries leads to iatrogenic ureteric stricture

Nerve supply

Sympathetic

  • Least splanchnic nerve (T12)
  • L1 and L2

Parasympathetic

  • Pelvic splanchnic nerves (S2, 3, 4)

Surface marking

4 cm lateral to spine of T12 to L2

Join posterior superior iliac spine (dimple)

Applied aspects - Intravenous pyelography (IVP)

X-ray examination of the kidneys, ureters and urinary bladder that uses iodinated contrast material injected into veins

In IVP, the contrast agent is given intravenously, allowed to be cleared by the kidneys and excreted through the urinary tract as part of the urine.

Cystoscopy - is a method of endoscopic visualization of the bladder

Applied aspects - identification of ureter during surgery

Ureter shows peristaltic activity that is much different from nearby pulsatile blood vessels.
